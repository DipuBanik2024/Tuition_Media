Help Center

General Questions

What is Tuition Media?

Tuition Media is a platform that connects students and parents with verified tutors for personalized in-person or online lessons.

Is it free to post a tuition requirement?

Yes! Students or guardians can post a tutor request completely free of charge.

For Students & Parents

How do I find a tutor?

Simply go to our “Post a Tuition” page, fill in your requirements, and we’ll match you with suitable tutors in your area.

Can I choose between home and online tutoring?

Yes, we offer both options. Just specify your preference while posting your tuition requirement.

For Tutors

How can I apply for tuition jobs?

After creating your tutor profile, you can browse tuition listings and apply directly from the dashboard.

Is there any verification process?

Yes, we verify tutor credentials such as education, experience, and identity for platform trust and safety.

Still Need Help?

Email us at support@tuitionmedia.com or message us through our Contact Form.
