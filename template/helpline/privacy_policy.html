Privacy Policy

Tuition Media is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address, etc.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interactions.
  • Form Submissions: Information submitted through tutor/student request forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect students with tutors.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To contact you with important updates or responses to your requests.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Registered tutors or students for matching purposes.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Please review it regularly to stay informed.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at privacy@tuitionmedia.com.
